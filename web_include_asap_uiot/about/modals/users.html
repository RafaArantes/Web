Rafael Timóteo

PhD in Telecommunications Engineering by Université de Rennes

Rafael Timóteo de Sousa Júnior was born in Campina Grande, Paraíba, Brazil, on June 24, 1961. He graduated in Electrical Engineering from Federal University of Paraíba in 1984 and got his Doctorate Degree in Telecommunications from University of Rennes in 1988. He worked as a software engineer in the company AQL (Rennes, 1988-1989) and as a network engineer in the company SGA (Brasília, 1989-1996). Since 1996, Rafael is a Network-Engineering Professor in the Electrical Engineering Department at University of Brasília. His field of study is computer networks and distributed systems and his current research interest is security and trust management for spontaneous self-organized networks.
