Coding Smart Devices

We integrate new devices in our architecture in a simple way. Constructing case uses for different technologies, writing a generic case use for each sensor, after that, involve into dynamic and abstract conversations with an iterative messaging system.

Conversations fluid by information. Device learning by the data collected in self network. Devices communicate theyself with our own protocol. Already more than 30 use cases, ready for more than 500 different devices.
